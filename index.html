Website Showcase

My personal website projects and designs.

Music App - Harmoni

Harmoni is a web app which highlights three popular artists and their top songs. Users can sign up for a newsletter, request new artists, and access each artist's YouTube and Instagram profiles. Clicking on a song banner directs users to its YouTube Music video, creating an engaging platform for music fans to connect with their favorite artists.

Parasite: A Stroll to Social Inequality

I created a website for my favorite movie, "Parasite," featuring a captivating main page, an about page detailing its plot and themes, and an honesty page examining the societal issues depicted in the film. This project reflects my design skills and appreciation for its impactful storytelling.

Interactive Form
